Our Journey

  • KTL Farming limited began in 2019 with a focus on the ovacado value chain, from growing to processing and marketing.
    •
  • It has grown into a vertically intergrated enterprise in western Kenya, supporting thousands of registered farmers through training and providing seedlings from its nursery.
    •
  • The company has experienced strong revenue growth and is working to expand its operations. focusing on sustainability, and accessing high value markets.
    •
